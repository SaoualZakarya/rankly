Common Questions
Everything you
want to know.
Is the first consultation really free?
+
Yes, completely. Your first 30-minute consultation with one of our clinicians is entirely free of charge and carries no obligation to proceed with treatment. We want you to feel informed and comfortable before making any decision.
How much does Invisalign cost in London?
+
Invisalign costs vary depending on the complexity of your case. At Ellison Dental, treatment typically ranges from £2,800 to £5,500. We offer 0% finance options to spread the cost with no interest. Exact pricing is provided in your written treatment plan after consultation.
Do you see nervous or anxious patients?
+
Absolutely. Dental anxiety is extremely common and our team is specially trained to help nervous patients feel at ease. We take everything at your pace, offer conscious sedation if needed, and have helped hundreds of anxious patients regain confidence in the dental chair.
How quickly can I get an appointment?
+
New patient consultations are typically available within 2–5 working days. Emergency appointments are available same-day or next-day. Simply call us or use the booking form below — our reception team will find a time that suits you.
Is private dentistry worth it vs NHS?
+
Private dentistry offers longer appointment times, a wider choice of treatments and materials, access to specialist expertise, and a more personalised experience. Our free guide covers this topic in detail — download it above for an honest comparison.

question?

Our patient coordinators are available Monday to Friday 8am–7pm and Saturday 9am–2pm. Call, email, or WhatsApp — we reply promptly.
